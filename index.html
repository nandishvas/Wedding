Our Story

  • First We Meet

    May 21, 2023

    Cladded in traditional attire of fire brick red and steel blue, she was a sight to behold. Nervous smiles turned into hearty laughter as the time flew. And in that simple room, as we conversed on and on... an instant connection was made.. the one to last for ages.

  • First Date

    June 10, 2023

    Very first rendezvous unfolded with a relaxing evening drive, through a gentle breeze as sun over statue of equality filled the sky with warm hue. Chats of shared interests and insecurities weaved a fabric of our personalities. Leisurely walk under canopy of twinkling city lights blossomed a budding connection. Promise of more such moments lingered in air as we parted ways.

  • In A Relationship

    Hitherto

    Nine months so far has unravelled a captivating novel by itself. Heartwarming chapters of shared laughter and joy are also bookmarked with those of conflicts and challenges mirroring the raw emotional versions of mutual perspectives. Navigating through pivotal turns, learning and growing together, theirs a lifetime ahead of us to explore.
